Education

My name is mrs Ruth Ekeh, a dedicated land surveyor with solid experience in field and office work including boundary surveys, Topographic surveys, mining survey, construction, drainage & sewage system surveys, land administration, Layout and As-built surveys, Geographic Information Systems, utility mapping, and volumetric surveys using state-of-the art survey equipment such as Total Station, GNSS units, 3D mobile mapping technology, and Unmanned aerial vehicles (aka drone) technology.

I am currently enrolled in a course-based master’s program in Geomatics engineering at the University of Calgary and taking courses in digital engineering which covers Python programming and machine learning, geospatial systems, Assets management, and project management, with a specialization in geospatial intelligence. Also, I am a Registered Land Surveyor with the Surveyors Council of Nigeria (SURCON). I have a bachelor’s degree in Surveying and Geoinformatics from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka Nigeria I offer an important experience with multiple industry software programs, including AutoCAD, QGIS, ArcGIS, Hi-Target geomatics, Franson Coordtrans Software, Merlin Land Administration Software, Golden Surfer, etc.
