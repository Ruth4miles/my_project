Educational Background

I have a bachelor’s degree in Surveying and Geoinformatics from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka Nigeria and also a Registered Land Surveyor with the Surveyors Council of Nigeria (SURCON).

I am currently enrolled in a course-based master’s program in Geomatics engineering at the University of Calgary and taking courses in digital engineering which covers Python programming and machine learning, geospatial systems, Assets management, and project management, with a specialization in geospatial intelligence.
